🎭 Tone Model

Twitter RoBERTa pretrained model was fine-tuned on news sentiment. Five tone classes were used: Very Negative, Negative, Neutral, Positive, Very Positive. Majority of articles exhibited a neutral tone.

📰 News Type Model

A semi-supervised approach combined manual labelling with BERT-based embeddings to categorize articles into News Reports, News Analysis, and Opinion pieces.

Political Leaning Analysis

Lexicon-based approach combined with TF-IDF and BERT embeddings to detect Left, Right, or Neutral leanings.

Overall Political Leaning Distribution
Political Leaning by Main Category

⚖️ Methodology

A curated political lexicon combined with TF-IDF term weighting and BERT sentence embeddings were used to assign a leaning score (Left / Neutral / Right) to each article.

📡 Data Sources

Articles from The Times of India and The Economic Times scraped using Beautiful Soup — covering Politics, Economy, Business, Health, Sports, Disaster, Science & Tech, and Culture.

Bias Score & Classification

Contextual bias scores computed via cosine similarity against a 3,000-term lexicon, with a Longformer+XGBoost hybrid classifier.

🤖 Hybrid Classifier

Longformer embeddings (for long document context) combined with article-level features (sentiment score, category, type) fed into a Voting Classifier (XGBoost + KNN).

📐 Bias Score Formula

Contextual bias score = cosine similarity of a document's TF-IDF vector against a curated lexicon of 3,000 bias-related terms, normalized per category.
